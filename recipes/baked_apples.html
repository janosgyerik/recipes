Baked Apples Ingredients: - 6 medium sized apples - 1.5 cup nuts pecans and walnuts, toasted - 1 tsp freshly grated orange zest - 1/4 cup dried cherries - 1/4 cup medjool dates - 2 tsp cinnamon - 1 teaspoon cardamom - 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg - 1/2 teaspoons ginger - 2 tablespoons orange juice - 1/4 cup maple syrup
